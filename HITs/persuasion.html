How persuasively can you write?

Suppose someone is just moving to your town and wants to check out some restaurants. Pick two restaurants that you think they should visit and one that you think they should avoid. As part of this study, you will write reviews of the three restaurants that convince your reader to go, or not go.

Bonuses are available for especially persuasive writing!

 

This study needs a smartphone with a good Internet connection, because you'll be using a different smartphone keyboard to write each review.
